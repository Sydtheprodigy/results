About Me

I am a registered massage therapist (RMT), with an advanced neuromuscular certification with the California massage therapy council. As a Neuromuscular Therapist, goals of care focus on improving mobility and restoring health. This is a highly specialized form of manual therapy that corrects the pain messages to the brain and dysfunction of the muscles. This is done by treating trigger points, muscle adhesions and fascial release. My sessions correct fascia, and reduce trigger points by using manual manipulation, stretching and compression techniques with range of motion.

Tigger Point Therapy, Myofascial Release, Pain Management and more.
